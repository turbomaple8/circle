Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the weekly rate?

Every room comes fully furnished with fast WiFi, all utilities (hydro, water, heating), weekly common-area cleaning, and access to building amenities like gyms, co-working spaces, and lounges. There are no hidden fees — what you see is what you pay.

How long can I stay?

We offer flexible terms from 1 month to 12+ months. Whether you need short-term housing while you settle into Toronto or a longer-term home for the school year, we have options for you.

Who lives at Circle?

Our community is a mix of university students, college students, young professionals, and newcomers to Toronto. Most residents are between 18 and 30 and value quiet, focused living with a social community nearby.

Do I need a credit check or guarantor?

No traditional credit check is required. We have a straightforward application process — apply online, and our team reviews your details within 48 hours. International students without Canadian credit history are welcome.

Can I view the property before committing?

Absolutely. We offer in-person viewings and virtual tours for international applicants who can't visit in advance. Book a viewing through our website or email us at info@circlestay.ca.

How does move-in work?

Once approved, you'll receive a move-in package with your key pickup details, house guidelines, and a welcome checklist. Most residents move in within a week of approval. Your room is fully furnished and ready — just bring your personal belongings.

Is it safe?

All our properties have secure key-fob or key-card access, and several locations offer 24/7 concierge service. We maintain community guidelines to ensure a respectful, safe environment for everyone. Our team is available around the clock for emergencies.

What's the difference between Circle and a regular apartment?

Unlike a solo apartment, Circle provides built-in community, fully furnished spaces, flexible terms with no long leases, and all-inclusive pricing. You skip the hassle of furniture shopping, utility setup, and WiFi installation — and you get housemates who share your values from day one.
