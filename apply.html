Before you apply

Do I need a credit check or Canadian guarantor?

No. Circle does not require a credit check or guarantor. Your application is assessed on your move-in date, location, and stay duration — not a credit score you may not have built yet.

What if I haven't decided on a location?

Select "Any / Flexible" and we'll recommend the best fit based on your move-in date, budget, and neighbourhood preferences.

What's the minimum stay?

One month. You can extend month-to-month without penalty, or commit to a longer stay for priority placement.

Is there a deposit required?

A standard security deposit applies upon lease signing, not application. The application itself is free and non-binding.

I'm an international student. Does that change anything?

No — most of our residents are international students or professionals. We work with letters of enrollment/employment from abroad and do not require Canadian credit history or in-country guarantor.
