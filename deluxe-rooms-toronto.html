Deluxe Rooms in Toronto: Premium Co-Living from C$315/Week

Queen bed, large desk, and premium wardrobe. Our most popular room type across all locations.

Deluxe rooms are Circle Co-Living's most popular room type, available at all four Toronto locations. Each deluxe room features a queen bed, large desk, and premium wardrobe in a spacious layout designed for comfort and productivity. At The Maddox in the Garden District, deluxe rooms start from C$315/week. The York in the Financial District offers deluxe rooms from C$370/week with access to premium amenities including a fitness centre, indoor pool, and sauna. The Queen in Queen West and The Yonge in the Downtown Core both offer deluxe rooms at C$385/week. Every deluxe room comes fully furnished with all utilities and WiFi included. Shared bathrooms are professionally maintained with regular cleaning. You also get full access to building amenities, common areas, and shared kitchens. Deluxe rooms are the sweet spot between affordability and comfort — more space and premium furnishings compared to basic rooms, at a price point that is still 40-55% less than a solo apartment in the same neighborhood. No credit check, flexible leases from 1 month, and professional management at every property.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who lives in coliving spaces in Toronto?+

Our community at The Maddox includes international students, young professionals, and newcomers to Toronto. It's a diverse mix of people who value intentional community over chaotic roommate situations.

What is coliving in Toronto?+

Coliving is a modern housing concept where you rent a private, furnished room in a professionally managed shared home. At The Maddox in Toronto, this means your own room plus shared kitchen, common areas, and amenities — all included in one weekly price from C$240.

Is coliving better than renting an apartment in Toronto?+

For many people, yes. Coliving saves you 38-60% compared to a solo apartment, includes all utilities and furniture, offers flexible leases, and provides a built-in community. No furniture shopping, no separate utility bills, no isolation.

Do I need a credit check for coliving?+

No. Circle Co-Living does not require Canadian credit history. Apply online, get approved within 48 hours, and move in as soon as this week.
